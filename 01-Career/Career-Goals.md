
# Career Goals

## Short-Term Goals (Next 6–12 Months)

- Transition into a DevOps Engineer role.
- Strengthen Linux and Infrastructure Automation expertise.
- Gain hands-on experience with Git, Jenkins, Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform, and AWS.
- Build a strong public GitHub portfolio.
- Improve interview preparation through consistent practice.

---

## Mid-Term Goals (1–3 Years)

- Become a highly skilled DevOps / Platform Engineer.
- Earn industry-recognized cloud and Kubernetes certifications.
- Design and automate production-grade infrastructure.
- Contribute to enterprise DevOps initiatives.

---

## Long-Term Goals (3–7 Years)

- Become a Senior DevOps / Site Reliability Engineer.
- Lead automation and cloud transformation projects.
- Mentor junior engineers.
- Design scalable, reliable, and secure cloud infrastructure.
